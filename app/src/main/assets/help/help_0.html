720度全景是通过把一组照片无缝拼接合成一张水平360度、上下90度的图像，拍摄这种图像以后可以用鼠标随意上下左右前后拖动观看整个四周3D效果，如身临其境般感觉。
720度全景图是一张比例为2：1的图片，是由多张高清照片拼合得到的图像，无限度地表现出真实场景的结构、质感和气氛，是对现实场景的真实再现。

720 degree panorama is a seamless mosaic of a group of photos to form a horizontal 360 degree, up and down 90 degree image. After shooting this image, you can use the mouse to drag up and down, left and right, back and forth to view the 3D effect of the whole surrounding area, which is like immersive feeling.
720 degree panorama is a 2:1 image, which is a combination of multiple high-definition photos. It shows the structure, texture and atmosphere of the real scene indefinitely, and is a real representation of the real scene.
