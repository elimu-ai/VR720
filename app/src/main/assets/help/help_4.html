1. 在主页或相册页面长按全景图，进入选择模式。
  2. 选择您需要分类的全景项目，点击右下角“添加到”。
  3. 您可以新建分类收藏夹或是添加到已有收藏夹。
  4. 这以后，您就可以在“分类收藏夹”页面中查看您已经分类完成的条目了。
  5. 或者，您也可以在已有分类收藏夹中点击“更多”，选择导入全景图，这也可以将指定的全景导入收藏夹中。
  1. Long press panorama on the home page or album page to enter the selection mode.
  2. Select the panoramas you want to classify and click "add to" in the lower right corner.
  3. You can create a new favorites or add it to an existing one.
  4. After that, you can view the items you have classified in the "My favorites" page.
  5. Alternatively, you can click "more" in the existing favorites and choose to import panorama, which can also import the specified panorama into the favorites.
